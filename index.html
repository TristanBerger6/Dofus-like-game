Rules

In order to catch the "Clofus pourpre", you will have to cross the labyrinth. To do this, you must anticipate the movements of the minogolem. Each turn, the minogolem will take a new state, and swap places with the minogolem of the indicated color. For example, if on the next turn, the minogolem has the red state, then it will swap places with the red minogolem. Be careful, after switching places, the minogolem will throw a rock in front of you to try to block your way !
You have 80HP to reach the "Clofus pourpre", each turn you will lose 1HP. You are also able to pass, and to quit the game if you're getting crazy ! Good luck!
